Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 7, 2025

Welcome to Econosave Minimart. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Econosave Minimart, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Delivery Information: Delivery address and contact details for order fulfillment
  • Order Information: Products purchased, order history, payment information, and transaction details
  • Profile Information: Optional profile details you choose to provide

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, pages viewed, and interaction patterns
  • Log Data: App crashes, error reports, and diagnostic information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Process and fulfill your orders, manage deliveries, and provide customer support
  • Account Management: Create and maintain your account, authenticate your identity, and manage your preferences
  • Communication: Send order confirmations, delivery updates, and important service notifications
  • Personalization: Provide personalized product recommendations and improve your shopping experience
  • Analytics: Analyze app usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Firebase Services

We use Google Firebase services to store and manage your data:

  • Firebase Authentication: Securely manages user accounts and authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores order data, product information, and user profiles
  • Firebase Storage: Hosts product images and related media files

Data stored in Firebase is protected by Google's security infrastructure and complies with industry-standard security practices.

3.2 Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/SSL
  • Secure authentication and password hashing
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Firestore security rules to protect user data

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Google Firebase for data storage and authentication
  • Delivery Partners: Only the information necessary to fulfill your orders (name, address, contact details)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authorities
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to users

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from promotional communications (service notifications may still be sent)

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active or as needed to provide services
  • Order History: Retained for 3 years for record-keeping and tax purposes
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request, except where legally required to retain

7. Children's Privacy

Econosave Minimart is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. Parents who believe their child has provided information should contact us.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our mobile app does not use traditional browser cookies. However, Firebase and analytics services may use similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze app performance and usage

9. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Expo: Expo Privacy Policy

These services have their own privacy policies governing data use.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app with an updated "Effective Date." Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy. For material changes, we will provide prominent notice.

13. Your Data Rights

  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information in the app
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt-out of optional data collection
